Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Flatten Dough
  2. Slather with Puree
  3. Sprinkle with cheese
  4. Distribute pepperoni
  5. Place in oven at 300degC for 5 minutes
  6. Eat!
