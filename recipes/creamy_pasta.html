Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cream Cheese
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Mushrooms
  • Knorr Stock

Steps

  1. Place pasta in boiling water for 10 minutes
  2. Drain pasta and place bag in pan
  3. Add cream cheese, knorr stock and mushrooms and stir on low heat for 2 minutes
  4. Add creme fraiche and cook for 3 minutes
  5. Eat!
