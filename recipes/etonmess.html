Ingredients

  • Meringue
  • Double Cream
  • Raspberries
  • Strawberries
  • Strawberry Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut up strawberries
  2. Place meringue in bowl
  3. Add all berries, drizzle of strawberry sauce and two table spoons of double cream
  4. Mix all ingredients together, crushing the meringue in the process
  5. Eat!
